1. Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in milk and egg.
  3. Add melted butter and stir.
  4. Heat pan and pour batter.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
